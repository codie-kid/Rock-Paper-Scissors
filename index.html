Game Rules

⏹️ Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beat Paper, and Paper beats Rock.

⏹️ Agree ahead of time whether you’ll count off “rock, paper, scissors, shoot” or just “rock, paper, scissors.”

⏹️ Use rock, paper, scissors to settle minor decisions or simply play to pass the time

⏹️ If both players lay down the same hand, each player lays down another hand
